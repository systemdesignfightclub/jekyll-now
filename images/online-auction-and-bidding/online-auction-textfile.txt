


ONLINE AUCTION





RESOURCES:
- similar to ad click aggregator from Alex Xu's book



REQUIREMENTS:
- allow items to be auctioned
- take bids
- 2 variations
    - fixed auction deadline
    - auction deadline is 60 minutes after the final bid


NUMBERS:
- 10M items per day
- 1B bids per day



how many items added per second?

10M / 100k -> 100 items per second





how many bids per second?

1B / 100k -> 10,000 bids per second
